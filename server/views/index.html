We Provide the Best Quality
Services Ever

FlowerShop Design is a full-service floral design and gift studio that has successfully served the greater Tel Aviv area since 2006. Conveniently located in midtown Tel Aviv, FlowerShop creates inspiring floral works of art for corporate and private events, weddings and charity galas. Proud to be a part of the culture of TLV, FlowerShop is a participant in the TLV City Couture Fashion Week, having created a collection of couture dresses entirely from flowers. A partial list of clients include: HSBC, Deutsche Bank, German Mission of the United Nations, Sotheby’s Realty, The Bone Marrow Foundation, Emilio Pucci, Jacob and Co., Chloe, L’Oreal and Estee Lauder.
